About Me

My name is Christopher Hylwa, I'm currently a junior at Tufts University, majoring in Computer Science. I live and work in Boston, MA.

My primary passion in life is to become a world-class game designer and developer, whatever that takes. I've worked on multiple small projects, including text adventure games, interactive fiction, 2-D puzzle games, and just last semester as a team of 5 making a 3-D action platforming game.
